Otter.ai — The Gold Standard

Otter.ai is the most popular AI meeting assistant. It provides real-time transcription, identifies different speakers, generates automated summaries, and extracts action items. OtterPilot joins Zoom, Google Meet, and Teams meetings automatically.

Fathom — Best Free Meeting AI

Fathom is completely free and provides excellent meeting recordings, transcriptions, and AI-generated summaries. It integrates with Zoom, Google Meet, and Microsoft Teams. The fact that it is free makes it the best starting point for anyone.
