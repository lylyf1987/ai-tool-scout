Otter.ai — The Gold Standard

Otter.ai is the most popular AI meeting assistant. It provides real-time transcription, identifies different speakers, generates automated summaries, and extracts action items. OtterPilot joins Zoom, Google Meet, and Teams meetings automatically.

Fathom — Best Free Meeting AI

Fathom is completely free and provides excellent meeting recordings, transcriptions, and AI-generated summaries. It integrates with Zoom, Google Meet, and Microsoft Teams. The fact that it is free makes it the best starting point for anyone.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best AI meeting assistant?

Otter.ai is the best overall AI meeting assistant with real-time transcription and speaker identification. Fathom is the best free option, and Fireflies AI is best for sales teams with CRM integration.

Can AI transcribe meetings in real-time?

Yes, tools like Otter.ai, Fathom, and tl;dv join your Zoom, Google Meet, or Teams calls and transcribe conversations in real-time, generating summaries and action items automatically.

Is there a free AI meeting assistant?

Fathom is completely free with unlimited recordings and AI summaries. Otter.ai offers a free tier with 300 minutes/month. tl;dv has a generous free tier for Google Meet and Zoom recordings.

Do AI meeting assistants work with Zoom?

Yes, all major AI meeting assistants (Otter.ai, Fathom, Fireflies, tl;dv) integrate with Zoom, Google Meet, and Microsoft Teams. Most work as bots that join your calls automatically.

Are AI meeting notes accurate?

AI meeting transcription accuracy is typically 85-95% depending on audio quality and accents. Most tools let you edit transcripts afterward. Summary accuracy for action items and key points is generally very good.
