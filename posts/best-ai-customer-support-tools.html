Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best AI customer support tool?

Intercom Fin is the best AI customer support tool, resolving 50% of tickets automatically. Zendesk AI is best for enterprise omnichannel support. Freshdesk AI is best for small teams on a budget.

Can AI replace customer support agents?

AI can handle 50-80% of routine customer inquiries automatically, but complex issues still require human agents. The best approach is AI-first with seamless human handoff for escalations, reducing team workload while maintaining quality.

How much does AI customer support cost?

AI customer support tools range from free tiers (Tidio, Freshdesk) to $39-55/month per agent for Intercom Fin and Zendesk AI. Enterprise plans with advanced AI features typically require custom pricing.

Does AI customer support actually work?

Yes, AI customer support tools achieve 50-85% automatic resolution rates for common questions. They work best for FAQs, order tracking, password resets, and product information. Complex or emotional issues still need human agents.

How do I add AI to my customer support?

Start by choosing a tool like Intercom Fin or Freshdesk AI. Upload your FAQs, help articles, and product documentation. Train the AI on your common support scenarios. Launch with AI handling simple questions and humans handling escalations.
