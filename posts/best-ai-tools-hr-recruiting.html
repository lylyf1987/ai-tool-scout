Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best AI tool for recruiting?

Greenhouse AI is the best AI recruiting platform for structured hiring. Lever AI is best for candidate relationship management. HireVue offers AI-powered video interviews and candidate assessment.

Can AI screen resumes?

Yes, AI resume screening tools scan applications for required skills, experience, and qualifications in seconds. They rank candidates by fit score, reducing time-to-hire by 40-60%. Always include human review to avoid bias.

How does AI help in HR?

AI helps HR teams by automating resume screening, scheduling interviews, generating job descriptions, analyzing employee engagement, predicting turnover risk, and streamlining onboarding processes.

Is AI recruiting biased?

AI recruiting tools can inherit biases from training data. Responsible vendors audit for bias and implement fairness constraints. Always combine AI screening with human review and diverse hiring panels for fair outcomes.

Can AI write job descriptions?

Yes, ChatGPT and specialized HR tools generate inclusive, compelling job descriptions. They optimize for diversity-friendly language, include relevant keywords, and format descriptions for job board compatibility.
