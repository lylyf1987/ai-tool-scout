What Is Google Gemini?

Gemini is Google's flagship AI model and assistant. It powers AI features across Google's product lineup and is available as a standalone chatbot at gemini.google.com. The latest Gemini 2.0 models are fast, capable, and deeply integrated with Google services. For more details, see our ChatGPT guide.

Key Features

  • Google Integration: Works with Gmail, Docs, Drive, Maps, YouTube, and Flights
  • Gems: Custom AI assistants for specific tasks (like ChatGPT's Custom GPTs)
  • Deep Research: Generates comprehensive research reports with citations
  • Image Generation: Create images using Imagen 3
  • Code Execution: Run code and analyze data
  • Grounding: Real-time Google Search integration for current information
  • 1M Token Context: Process extremely long documents (Gemini 1.5 Pro)

Performance

Writing: Gemini produces good-quality writing, though it can be slightly less natural than Claude or ChatGPT. It excels at formatting and structuring content. 8/10 For more details, see our Gemini vs ChatGPT guide.

Research: With Google Search grounding and Deep Research, Gemini is exceptional for research tasks. The Deep Research feature creates detailed, cited reports that save hours of manual work. 9/10 For more details, see our Perplexity AI guide.

Google Integration: This is Gemini's killer feature. Reading your Gmail, summarizing Docs, analyzing Sheets — if you live in Google's ecosystem, Gemini is unmatched. 10/10

Speed: Gemini is notably fast, especially the Flash models. Responses feel snappier than competitors. 9/10

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Google Gemini better than ChatGPT?

Google Gemini has a larger context window (1M tokens) and better Google ecosystem integration. ChatGPT has more versatile output, a larger plugin ecosystem, and better creative writing. Choose Gemini for Google Workspace users, ChatGPT for general AI use.

Is Gemini free to use?

Yes, Google Gemini has a generous free tier with access to the Gemini model. Gemini Advanced with the most capable model costs $20/month and is bundled with Google One AI Premium including 2TB Google Drive storage.

What can Gemini do?

Gemini can answer questions, analyze images and documents, write content, code, integrate with Gmail/Docs/Drive, search YouTube, generate images, and provide real-time Google Search results.

Does Gemini work with Google Workspace?

Yes, Gemini integrates with Google Workspace to help write emails in Gmail, create docs, build slides, analyze Sheets data, and summarize Drive files. This requires a Gemini Business or Enterprise add-on for Workspace.

What is Gemini 1.5 Pro?

Gemini 1.5 Pro is Googles most capable AI model with a 1 million token context window. It can process entire books, codebases, or hours of video in a single conversation, making it ideal for large-scale analysis tasks.

Final Verdict

Google Gemini is the best AI assistant for anyone heavily invested in the Google ecosystem. Its Deep Research feature and Google integrations are genuinely useful. While it's not quite as precise as Claude or as versatile as ChatGPT, it's a strong contender that keeps getting better. 4.5 out of 5 stars.

If you use Google Workspace daily, Gemini Advanced is a no-brainer. The integration alone is worth the subscription.
