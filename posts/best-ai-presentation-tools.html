Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best AI presentation maker?

Gamma AI is the best AI presentation maker, generating beautiful slides from text prompts in seconds. Canva AI is best for design flexibility, and Tome AI is best for storytelling presentations.

Can AI create a PowerPoint presentation?

Yes, tools like Gamma AI, SlidesAI, and Tome AI can generate complete presentations from a text prompt or outline. They create slides with layouts, images, and content automatically.

Is Gamma AI free?

Gamma AI offers a free tier with 400 AI credits. Paid plans start at $10/month for unlimited AI generation, custom fonts, and export to PowerPoint and PDF formats.

What is the fastest way to make a presentation?

Use an AI presentation tool like Gamma AI or Tome AI. Write a prompt describing your topic, and the AI generates a complete slide deck in 30-60 seconds. Then customize the content and design.

Can AI help with Google Slides?

Yes, SlidesAI is a Google Slides add-on that generates AI presentations directly in Google Slides. It creates slide content, layouts, and images from a text prompt without leaving Google Slides.
