Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best AI tool for therapy practices?

SimplePractice is the best AI-enhanced practice management platform. Therabill provides AI billing assistance. Notero AI helps with AI-powered clinical notes. Owl Practice offers AI scheduling and documentation.

Can AI write therapy notes?

Yes, AI tools like Mentalyc and Notero generate SOAP-style clinical notes from session recordings or summaries. Always review AI-generated notes for accuracy and ensure compliance with documentation standards.

How can therapists use AI?

Therapists use AI for clinical note generation, practice management, scheduling optimization, billing automation, treatment planning resources, and professional development through research summaries.

Is AI therapy practice management HIPAA compliant?

Yes, platforms like SimplePractice and Owl Practice are HIPAA-compliant with secure data handling. Always verify compliance certification before using any AI tool with patient health information.

Can AI help with therapy billing?

Yes, AI billing tools automate insurance claim submission, verify coverage, detect coding errors, track payments, and reduce claim denials by ensuring accurate CPT codes and documentation.
