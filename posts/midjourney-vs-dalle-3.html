Choosing between Midjourney and DALL-E 3 for AI image generation? This comprehensive comparison breaks down everything you need to know about these two leading AI art tools.

Both Midjourney v6 and DALL-E 3 represent the cutting edge of AI image generation, but they excel in different areas. Midjourney produces stunning artistic imagery with incredible detail and aesthetics, while DALL-E 3 excels at following complex prompts and rendering text within images. For more options, see our best AI art generators guide.

Image Quality Comparison

Midjourney v6 produces images with superior artistic quality — richer colors, better composition, and more photorealistic details. DALL-E 3 generates cleaner, more accurate representations of prompts but can sometimes lack the artistic flair that makes Midjourney images stand out. For professional design work, Midjourney is generally preferred.

Prompt Accuracy

DALL-E 3 wins on prompt accuracy. It follows complex, multi-element prompts faithfully and handles spatial relationships better. Midjourney v6 has improved significantly but still interprets prompts more artistically, sometimes adding creative flourishes that diverge from the exact request.

Text Rendering

DALL-E 3 is the clear winner for text rendering in images. It can accurately render words, logos, and typography within generated images. Midjourney v6 has improved text rendering but still struggles with longer phrases and consistent typography.

Pricing Comparison

Midjourney starts at $10/month for ~200 images, with plans up to $120/month for unlimited generation. DALL-E 3 is included with ChatGPT Plus at $20/month, which gives you access to GPT-4o as well. The DALL-E 3 option offers better value if you also need an AI chatbot.

Ease of Use

Midjourney operates primarily through Discord, which can be intimidating for new users. DALL-E 3 is accessible through the ChatGPT interface, making it much more approachable. However, Midjourney's website now offers a more streamlined experience for browsing and generating images.

Commercial Usage Rights

Both tools grant commercial usage rights to paid subscribers. Midjourney's paid plans include commercial terms, while DALL-E 3 images generated through ChatGPT Plus can be used commercially. Always check the latest terms before using generated images for commercial projects.

The Verdict

Choose Midjourney if: You want the most artistic, visually stunning AI images and don't mind the Discord-based interface. Best for designers, artists, and creative professionals.

Choose DALL-E 3 if: You need accurate prompt following, text rendering, and an easy-to-use interface. Best for marketers, content creators, and anyone who also wants a powerful AI chatbot.
