Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best AI transcription tool?

Otter.ai is the best AI transcription tool for meetings. Rev AI is best for high-accuracy general transcription. Descript is best for transcription plus editing. Whisper by OpenAI is best for free open-source transcription.

How accurate is AI transcription?

AI transcription accuracy is typically 90-98% for clear audio. Rev AI achieves up to 99% accuracy. Accuracy decreases with background noise, multiple speakers, accents, and technical vocabulary.

Is there a free AI transcription tool?

Otter.ai offers 300 free minutes per month. OpenAI Whisper is free and open source. Google Docs Voice Typing provides free real-time transcription. These are great for basic transcription needs.

Can AI transcribe YouTube videos?

Yes, tools like Otter.ai, Rev, and Whisper can transcribe YouTube videos from the audio. YouTube also provides auto-generated captions using AI that can be downloaded or edited.

Can AI transcribe in real-time?

Yes, tools like Otter.ai, Google Live Transcribe, and Apple Dictation provide real-time AI transcription. They display text as people speak, useful for meetings, lectures, and accessibility.
