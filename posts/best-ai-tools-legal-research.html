Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best AI legal research tool?

CoCounsel by Casetext (Thomson Reuters) is the best AI legal research assistant. Lexis+ AI is best for comprehensive legal database searching. Westlaw Precision with AI provides advanced case law analysis.

Can AI find case law?

Yes, AI legal research tools search millions of cases to find relevant precedents based on your factual scenario. They summarize key holdings, identify favorable and unfavorable authority, and draft research memos.

Is AI legal research accurate?

AI legal research tools are generally accurate but can hallucinate case citations. Always verify cited cases exist and read the actual opinions. Professional tools like CoCounsel and Lexis+ have safeguards against hallucinations.

How much does AI legal research cost?

Professional AI legal research platforms like Lexis+ and Westlaw range from $200-800/month depending on features. ChatGPT provides basic legal research assistance for free but requires careful verification.

Can ChatGPT do legal research?

ChatGPT can help with basic legal research, explain legal concepts, and suggest search terms. However, it can fabricate case citations. Always verify any legal authority ChatGPT cites against primary sources.
