When to Choose ChatGPT

  • You want the most versatile, all-purpose AI assistant
  • You need image generation (DALL-E built in)
  • You use Custom GPTs and plugins
  • You want voice conversations on mobile
  • You value a large ecosystem of integrations

When to Choose Claude

  • You need the best reasoning and analysis
  • You work with long documents (200K context)
  • Coding is your primary use case
  • You want more precise, less verbose output
  • You value safety and ethical AI practices

Our Recommendation

For most people: Start with ChatGPT — it's the most versatile and has the broadest feature set.

For professionals: Choose Claude — it's superior for complex reasoning, coding, and precise writing.

Best option: Use both. Many power users subscribe to both services and use each for its strengths. ChatGPT for creative tasks and multimodal features, Claude for analysis and coding.
