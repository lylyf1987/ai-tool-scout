Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best AI grammar checker?

Grammarly is the best AI grammar checker for comprehensive writing improvement. ProWritingAid is best for detailed writing analysis. LanguageTool is best for multilingual grammar checking.

Is Grammarly free?

Grammarly offers a free tier that checks spelling, basic grammar, and punctuation. Grammarly Premium at $12/month adds advanced suggestions for tone, clarity, engagement, and delivery.

Can AI fix my grammar?

Yes, AI grammar tools automatically detect and correct grammar, spelling, punctuation, and style issues in real-time as you type in browsers, documents, and email clients.

What is better than Grammarly?

ProWritingAid offers more detailed writing reports and is cheaper for long-form writers. LanguageTool supports more languages. QuillBot is better for paraphrasing. Choose based on your primary writing needs.

Can AI grammar checkers detect plagiarism?

Grammarly Premium includes plagiarism detection comparing text against billions of web pages. Turnitin is the gold standard for academic plagiarism detection. Most free grammar tools do not include plagiarism checking.
