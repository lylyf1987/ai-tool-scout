Frequently Asked Questions

Can AI help with mental health?

AI tools like Woebot and Wysa provide helpful CBT-based exercises and conversational support for mild stress and anxiety. They supplement but do not replace licensed therapy for clinical conditions.

What is the best AI therapy app?

Woebot is the best AI therapy chatbot using CBT techniques. Wysa is best for anxiety and mood tracking. Youper is best for AI-guided emotional health monitoring. These are supplements to professional care.

Is AI therapy effective?

AI therapy tools show effectiveness for mild to moderate stress, anxiety, and mood management. Research shows 50-60% symptom improvement for users. They are not replacements for licensed therapists for serious conditions.

Can AI replace a therapist?

No, AI cannot replace a licensed therapist for clinical conditions. AI tools are helpful for self-help, skill building, and between-session support, but complex mental health issues require professional human care.

Is there a free AI mental health app?

Woebot offers a free tier with CBT conversations. Wysa provides free basic mood tracking and exercises. Mindshift CBT is a free anxiety management app. These provide accessible mental health support at no cost.
