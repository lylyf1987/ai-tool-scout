Woebot — Best AI Therapy Chatbot

Woebot uses cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT) principles to help users manage anxiety, depression, and stress. It checks in daily, teaches coping strategies, and tracks mood patterns over time. Developed by clinical psychologists at Stanford.

ChatGPT — Free Mental Health Support

ChatGPT can help with journaling prompts, CBT exercises, breathing techniques, guided meditation scripts, and understanding mental health concepts. Not a therapist, but useful for daily wellness practices.
