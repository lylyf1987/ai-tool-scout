What Is Perplexity AI?

Perplexity AI is an AI-powered search engine and research assistant that provides cited, sourced answers to your questions. It combines the power of large language models with real-time web search to deliver accurate, up-to-date information.

Key Features

  • Real-Time Search: Access current information from the web
  • Deep Research: Generate comprehensive research reports on any topic
  • File Upload: Analyze uploaded documents
  • Collections: Organize research into reusable folders
  • Focus Modes: Search academic papers, YouTube, Reddit, and more
  • Spaces: Collaborative research workspaces

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Perplexity AI better than Google?

Perplexity AI provides direct answers with cited sources, making it better for research than traditional search engines. Google is better for shopping, local searches, and browsing multiple sources. Use Perplexity for answers, Google for exploration.

Is Perplexity AI free?

Perplexity offers a free tier with AI-powered search and cited sources. Perplexity Pro at $20/month adds access to GPT-4o and Claude models, unlimited Pro searches, file analysis, and image generation.

How is Perplexity different from ChatGPT?

Perplexity is a search engine that cites sources for every answer, while ChatGPT is a general AI assistant. Perplexity is better for factual research with citations. ChatGPT is better for creative tasks, coding, and conversation.

Can Perplexity write essays?

Perplexity can help research and outline essays with cited sources, but it focuses on factual answers rather than long-form writing. For essay writing, ChatGPT or Claude are better choices. Use Perplexity for the research phase.

Does Perplexity show sources?

Yes, Perplexity always shows numbered source citations with clickable links for every answer. You can verify information directly from the source, making it one of the most transparent AI tools available.
