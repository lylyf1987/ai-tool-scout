Canva AI — Design for Everyone

Canva AI generates complete designs from text descriptions. Describe what you need and it creates social media posts, presentations, flyers, business cards, and more. The AI handles layout, typography, colors, and even suggests images. The free tier is generous enough for most casual users.

Figma AI — Professional UI Design

Figma's AI features auto-generate UI layouts, suggest component variations, rename layers intelligently, and remove backgrounds from images. For professional UI/UX designers, Figma with AI is the industry standard.
