Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best AI video generator?

Runway ML is the best overall AI video generator with its Gen-3 Alpha model for creative professionals. Synthesia is best for AI avatar videos, and Pika is best for quick edits and effects.

Can AI generate videos from text?

Yes, Runway ML, Pika, and Luma Dream Machine generate short videos from text prompts. Current tools produce 4-16 second clips. Quality is improving rapidly with each new model release.

Is there a free AI video generator?

Luma Dream Machine offers free daily generations. Pika has a free tier with limited credits. Runway ML provides a free trial. Most tools offer some free access to try before subscribing.

How long are AI-generated videos?

Current AI video generators produce clips between 4 and 16 seconds. Runway Gen-3 can generate up to 16 seconds, while Pika and Luma typically produce 4-5 second clips. You can combine multiple clips for longer videos.

Can AI create videos with people?

Yes, Synthesia and HeyGen create videos with realistic AI avatars that speak your script in 120+ languages. Runway ML and Pika can generate videos of people from text prompts, though quality varies.
