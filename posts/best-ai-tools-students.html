Best AI for Essay Writing

Claude produces the most nuanced, well-structured essays. Use it for brainstorming, outlining, and getting feedback on your writing. Always cite your sources and use AI as a learning aid, not a replacement for your own thinking.

Best AI for Math & Science

Photomath (by Google) scans and solves math problems step-by-step. Wolfram Alpha handles complex calculations. And ChatGPT with Code Interpreter can solve and explain complex problems.

Best AI for Research

Perplexity AI is the best for academic research because it cites its sources. Consensus specializes in finding and summarizing academic papers.

Remember: AI tools should enhance your learning, not replace it. Always understand and verify the output, and check your institution's guidelines on AI use.
