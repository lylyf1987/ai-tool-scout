Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best AI note-taking app?

Notion AI is the best all-in-one note-taking app with AI writing and search features. Otter.ai is best for meeting notes, and Obsidian is best for privacy-focused local-first note-taking.

Can AI take notes during meetings?

Yes, Otter.ai and Fireflies AI automatically join your meetings, transcribe conversations, and generate AI-powered summaries with action items. No manual note-taking required.

Is Notion AI free?

Notion has a generous free tier, but AI features cost an additional $10/month per user. The free tier includes unlimited pages and blocks for personal use.

What is the best note-taking app for students?

Notion Free is the best for students with unlimited pages, databases, and collaboration. It includes a free education tier with extra features. Obsidian is great for research-heavy students who value privacy.

Can AI organize my notes automatically?

Yes, apps like Mem AI and Notion AI can automatically organize, tag, and link your notes. Mem AI uses AI to surface relevant notes when you need them, while Notion AI helps search across your workspace.
