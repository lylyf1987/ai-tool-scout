What Google Says About AI Content

Google has clarified that AI-generated content is acceptable as long as it is helpful and created for users, not solely for search engine manipulation. The key factors remain: expertise, experience, authoritativeness, and trustworthiness (E-E-A-T).

The Best Approach: AI + Human

The most successful content strategy in 2026 uses AI for speed and scale while adding human expertise, personal experience, and editorial oversight. Tools like Claude and ChatGPT are excellent for first drafts, outlines, and research, but human writers add the authenticity and expertise that audiences value most.
