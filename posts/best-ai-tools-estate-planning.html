Frequently Asked Questions

Can AI help with estate planning?

AI can help organize assets, draft basic estate planning documents, explain legal concepts, and create checklists. However, estate planning involves complex legal requirements that should be reviewed by a qualified attorney.

What is the best AI tool for estate planning?

Trust & Will offers AI-assisted estate planning document generation. LegalZoom provides AI-guided will creation. ChatGPT helps research estate planning concepts and create asset inventories for attorney review.

Can AI write a will?

AI can help draft a basic will template, but estate planning laws vary by jurisdiction. Always have AI-generated wills reviewed by a licensed attorney to ensure legal validity and proper execution in your state.

Is AI estate planning legally binding?

AI-generated estate documents may not be legally binding without attorney review. Each state has specific requirements for will execution. Use AI for preparation and organization, then consult an estate planning attorney for final documents.

How much does AI estate planning cost?

AI estate planning tools like Trust & Will charge $100-400 for document templates. This is significantly less than attorney fees ($1000-5000). However, complex estates still benefit from professional legal guidance.
