Roam Around AI — Best AI Itinerary Builder

Roam Around creates detailed day-by-day itineraries based on your destination, dates, interests, and budget. It suggests optimal routes between attractions, recommends restaurants, and adjusts plans based on opening hours and travel times.

ChatGPT — Free Travel Planning Advisor

ChatGPT is an excellent free travel advisor. It helps create custom itineraries, suggests off-the-beaten-path attractions, translates phrases, estimates budgets, compares destinations, and provides cultural tips for any country.
