What Is Runway ML?

Runway ML is the leading AI video generation platform, enabling anyone to create and edit videos using artificial intelligence. From text-to-video generation to AI-powered editing tools, Runway is at the forefront of AI video technology.

Key Features

  • Gen-3 Alpha: Generate realistic videos from text prompts
  • Image-to-Video: Animate static images into video clips
  • Motion Brush: Add specific motion to parts of an image
  • AI Green Screen: Remove backgrounds without a physical green screen
  • Text-to-Image: Generate images from descriptions
  • Video Editing Suite: AI-powered editing tools
  • Expand Video: Extend video beyond original frame

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Runway ML?

Runway ML is the leading AI video generation and editing platform. Its Gen-3 Alpha model creates high-quality videos from text prompts and images, widely used by filmmakers and creative professionals.

Is Runway ML free?

Runway ML offers a free tier with 125 credits. Paid plans start at $15/month for 625 credits. Each video generation uses credits based on length and quality settings.

How long are Runway ML videos?

Runway Gen-3 Alpha generates videos up to 16 seconds long. You can extend clips by generating additional segments. Most users create 4-10 second clips for social media and creative projects.

Can Runway ML edit existing videos?

Yes, Runway ML includes AI video editing features like background removal, motion tracking, color grading, green screen, blur effects, and AI-powered object removal from video footage.

Is Runway ML good for filmmakers?

Yes, Runway ML is used by professional filmmakers for pre-visualization, concept art generation, B-roll creation, style transfers, and creative effects. It was used in the production of the Oscar-nominated film Everything Everywhere All at Once.
