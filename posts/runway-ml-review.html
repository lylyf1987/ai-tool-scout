Runway ML Review (2026): The Best AI Video Generator?

📅 March 2026 ⏱️ 9 min read ⭐ 4.3/5

What Is Runway ML?

Runway ML is the leading AI video generation platform, enabling anyone to create and edit videos using artificial intelligence. From text-to-video generation to AI-powered editing tools, Runway is at the forefront of AI video technology.

Key Features

  • Gen-3 Alpha: Generate realistic videos from text prompts
  • Image-to-Video: Animate static images into video clips
  • Motion Brush: Add specific motion to parts of an image
  • AI Green Screen: Remove backgrounds without a physical green screen
  • Text-to-Image: Generate images from descriptions
  • Video Editing Suite: AI-powered editing tools
  • Expand Video: Extend video beyond original frame

Performance

Video Generation: Runway's Gen-3 Alpha produces impressive video clips. Quality varies by prompt, but the best results are genuinely remarkable. Score: 8.5/10

Editing Tools: The AI editing suite is powerful and intuitive. Green screen removal and motion brush are particularly useful. Score: 9/10

Speed: Video generation takes 1-5 minutes depending on length and complexity. Score: 8/10

Pros

  • Best AI video generation available
  • Powerful editing suite
  • Continuously improving models
  • Good for both professionals and beginners

Cons

  • Credits are expensive for heavy use
  • Video length limited to ~10 seconds
  • Inconsistent quality across prompts
  • Steep learning curve for best results
