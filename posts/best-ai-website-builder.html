Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best AI website builder?

Wix ADI is the best AI website builder for ease of use with drag-and-drop editing. Framer AI is best for modern design, and Hostinger AI is best for budget-friendly websites starting at $3/month.

Can AI build a website for free?

Wix offers a free plan with AI website generation (includes Wix branding). Framer has a free tier for basic sites. Hostinger and 10Web require paid plans but are very affordable starting at $3-10/month.

How fast can AI build a website?

AI website builders can create a complete website in 1-5 minutes. You answer a few questions about your business, and the AI generates a full site with pages, content, images, and contact forms.

Are AI-built websites good for SEO?

AI website builders like Wix and Framer generate SEO-friendly sites with proper heading structures, meta tags, and mobile responsiveness. However, you should customize SEO settings and add unique content for best results.

Can I customize an AI-generated website?

Yes, all AI website builders let you fully customize the generated design. You can change colors, fonts, layouts, add pages, modify content, and integrate third-party tools after the initial AI generation.
