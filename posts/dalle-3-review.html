What Is DALL-E 3?

DALL-E 3 is OpenAI image generation model, accessible through ChatGPT. It is known for excellent prompt adherence, text rendering in images, and seamless integration with ChatGPT workflow.

Key Features

  • Prompt Accuracy: Best-in-class at following detailed prompts
  • Text in Images: Can render readable text within generated images
  • ChatGPT Integration: Generate images within ChatGPT conversations
  • Style Options: Natural and vivid style presets
  • Safety Filters: Built-in content safety guardrails

Performance

Image Quality: DALL-E 3 produces clean, well-composed images. While not as artistic as Midjourney, it is more reliable for specific prompts. Score: 8.5/10

Text Rendering: The best AI image generator for including readable text in images. Score: 9/10

Prompt Accuracy: Follows complex, detailed prompts better than competitors. Score: 9.5/10

Pros

  • Best prompt adherence of any image AI
  • Excellent text rendering in images
  • Integrated with ChatGPT (convenient)
  • Included with ChatGPT Plus ($20/mo)

Cons
