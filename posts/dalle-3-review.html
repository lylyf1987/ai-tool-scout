What Is DALL-E 3?

DALL-E 3 is OpenAI image generation model, accessible through ChatGPT. It is known for excellent prompt adherence, text rendering in images, and seamless integration with ChatGPT workflow.

Key Features

  • Prompt Accuracy: Best-in-class at following detailed prompts
  • Text in Images: Can render readable text within generated images
  • ChatGPT Integration: Generate images within ChatGPT conversations
  • Style Options: Natural and vivid style presets
  • Safety Filters: Built-in content safety guardrails

Frequently Asked Questions

What is DALL-E 3?

DALL-E 3 is OpenAIs latest AI image generator integrated into ChatGPT. It creates images from text descriptions with excellent prompt understanding and text rendering capabilities.

Is DALL-E 3 free?

DALL-E 3 is included with ChatGPT Plus at $20/month (limited generations per day). Microsoft Copilot offers free DALL-E 3 image generation through Bing Image Creator with daily limits.

How does DALL-E 3 compare to Midjourney?

DALL-E 3 follows prompts more accurately and renders text better. Midjourney produces more artistic and aesthetically pleasing results. Choose DALL-E 3 for accuracy, Midjourney for artistic quality.

Can I use DALL-E 3 images commercially?

Yes, images generated with DALL-E 3 through paid ChatGPT Plus can be used commercially. Free Bing Image Creator generations also include commercial rights. Always check current terms before commercial use.

What is the resolution of DALL-E 3 images?

DALL-E 3 generates images at 1024x1024, 1792x1024, or 1024x1792 pixels depending on the aspect ratio selected. This is suitable for web use and small prints.
