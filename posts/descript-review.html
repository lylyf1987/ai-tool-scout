Descript Review (2026): The Best AI Video & Audio Editor?

📅 March 2026 ⏱️ 8 min read ⭐ 4.7/5

What Is Descript?

Descript is an AI-powered media editor that lets you edit audio and video by editing text. Delete words in the transcript and they are removed from the media. It also offers AI-powered filler word removal, studio sound enhancement, screen recording, and automatic captions.

Key Features

  • Text-Based Editing: Edit audio/video by editing the transcript
  • Filler Word Removal: Automatically remove ums, ahs, and filler words
  • Studio Sound: AI audio enhancement for professional quality
  • Eye Contact: AI-corrected eye contact for videos
  • Green Screen: AI background removal without green screen
  • Overdub: Clone your voice to fix mistakes without re-recording
  • AI Captions: Automatic caption generation
  • Screen Recording: Built-in screen and camera recording

Performance

Text Editing: Editing media by editing text is revolutionary. It is intuitive, fast, and dramatically speeds up post-production. Score: 10/10

AI Features: Filler word removal and Studio Sound are genuinely useful. Eye contact correction is good but occasionally noticeable. Score: 9/10

Video Editing: Full multi-track video editing with transitions, titles, and effects. Not as deep as Premiere but sufficient for most content creators. Score: 8.5/10

Pros

  • Text-based editing is game-changing
  • Excellent AI audio enhancement
  • Automatic filler word removal
  • Free plan available
  • All-in-one recording and editing
  • Overdub voice cloning

Cons

  • Can be slow with large projects
  • Free plan has limited exports
  • Not ideal for complex video projects
  • Requires good internet connection
