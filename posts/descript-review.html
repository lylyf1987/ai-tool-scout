What Is Descript?

Descript is an AI-powered media editor that lets you edit audio and video by editing text. Delete words in the transcript and they are removed from the media. It also offers AI-powered filler word removal, studio sound enhancement, screen recording, and automatic captions.

Key Features

  • Text-Based Editing: Edit audio/video by editing the transcript
  • Filler Word Removal: Automatically remove ums, ahs, and filler words
  • Studio Sound: AI audio enhancement for professional quality
  • Eye Contact: AI-corrected eye contact for videos
  • Green Screen: AI background removal without green screen
  • Overdub: Clone your voice to fix mistakes without re-recording
  • AI Captions: Automatic caption generation
  • Screen Recording: Built-in screen and camera recording

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Descript good for podcast editing?

Descript is excellent for podcast editing with its text-based editing approach. You edit audio by deleting text in the transcript. It automatically removes filler words, creates clips, and generates show notes.

Is Descript free?

Descript offers a free tier with 1 hour of transcription per month and basic editing. Paid plans start at $24/month for the Hobbyist plan with 24 hours of transcription and AI features like filler word removal.

Can Descript remove background noise?

Yes, Descript includes Studio Sound AI that removes background noise and enhances audio quality to studio-level. It works on existing recordings to clean up audio recorded in less-than-ideal environments.

How does Descript text-based editing work?

Descript transcribes your audio or video automatically. You see the transcript as a text document. Delete words from the transcript, and the corresponding audio/video is removed. It makes editing as easy as editing a document.

Can Descript clone my voice?

Yes, Descripts Overdub feature creates an AI clone of your voice. You can then type new words and Descript generates them in your voice, allowing you to fix mistakes without re-recording.
