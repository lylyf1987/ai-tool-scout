Frequently Asked Questions

How do I build an AI chatbot?

Use a no-code chatbot builder like Chatbase, Botpress, or Voiceflow. Upload your data (website, docs, FAQs), customize the personality, and embed the chatbot on your site with a single code snippet.

What is the best free AI chatbot builder?

Chatbase offers a free tier with 30 messages/month. Botpress has an open-source core that is free. Voiceflow offers a free tier good for prototyping chatbot flows.

Can I train an AI chatbot on my own data?

Yes, tools like Chatbase, Custom GPT, and Dante AI let you train chatbots on your website content, PDFs, documents, and FAQs. The AI uses your data to answer customer questions accurately.

How much does an AI chatbot cost?

AI chatbot builders range from free tiers to $30-500/month for business plans. Chatbase starts at $19/month, Botpress at $495/month for teams, and Voiceflow at $50/month for pro features.

Can AI chatbots replace human support?

AI chatbots can handle 50-80% of routine customer questions automatically. For complex issues, they escalate to human agents. The best approach is AI-first with seamless human handoff.
